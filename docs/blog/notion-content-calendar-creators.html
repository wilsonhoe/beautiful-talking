What a Content Calendar Should Actually Do

A proper content calendar isn't just a calendar. It's a complete publishing system that handles:

  5. Analysis — See what's working so you double down

How I Use It: My Weekly Workflow

Monday: Ideation (30 min)

Dump every idea into the Content Pipeline. No filtering. Tag by platform and topic. The Hashtag Bank suggests tags automatically.

Tuesday-Wednesday: Creation (2 hrs)

Pick top ideas from the pipeline. Use Post Templates to draft quickly. Move cards to Draft when done.

Thursday: Scheduling (30 min)

Drag drafts onto the Weekly Planner. Assign dates and times. Move cards to Scheduled.

Friday: Review (20 min)

Check the Engagement Tracker for this week's published posts. What performed? What flopped? Update the Analytics Dashboard.
